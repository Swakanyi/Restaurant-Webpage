Our Story - The History of Dine with CE&E

CE&E Restaurant was born out of a shared dream between three siblings - Chloe,Emma and Ethan; who believed that dining should feel like coming home, even in the most elegant of settings.

Growing up in different corners of the world, the trio spent their early years exploring vibrant oood cultures, from Chloe's rustic countryside kitchen, to Emma's fine dining experiences abroad, to Ethan's street food inspired flavors. It was during their travels and gatherings that the idea for CE&E Restaurant began to simmer. In 2022, they brought that dream to life; opening a space where luxury meets warmth, where families could gather, friends could toast and every meal felt like a memory in the making.

Set in the heart of the city but designed with the charm of a countryside retreat, Dine with CE&E combines elegant interiors with a comforting homestyle menu. Today, CE&E is more than just a restaurant - a place of connection, community and culinary celebration.

Our Mission

At Dine with CE&E, our mission is to craft a world-class dining experience that blends luxury, warmth and rustic charm.

We are committed to serving thoughtfully prepared dishes inspired by tradition, guided by innovation and delivered with genuine hospitality. We believe that good food should bring people together, tell a story and leave a lasting impression - whether it's a quiet dinner for two or a joyous family gathering.

From the first welcome to the final bite, our goal is to make every guest feel at home.

Meet The Team Behind Dine with CE&E

At the heart of Dine With CE&E is a team bound with friendship, passion and purpose. What began as a shared dream between three food lovers has grown into a community of chefs, hosts and creatives working together to bring you unforgettable dining experiences.

Chloe - Creative Director

The heart behind the ambience. Chloe brings her rustic countryside upbringing and love for interior design to create the warm, inviting space our guests love. She curates the atmosphere, seasonal decor and aesthetic touches that make Dine With CE&E feel like home.

Emma - Executive Chef

The soul behind the menu. Emma trained in fine dining kitchens around the world and she now blends global flavors with local ingredients to craft our signature dishes. Her creativity and attention to detail turns meals into memories.

Ethan - Operations Manager

The mind behind the experience. Ethan ensures smooth dat-to-day operations, staff training and customer service. With a background in hospitality and a deep love for people, Ethan makes sure every guest leaves satisfied and smiling.
